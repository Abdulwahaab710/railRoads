About Rail Roads


  • Overview

  • railRoads is a choose your own adventure game about university life. The game is designed to determine how certain decision making behaviours influence the experience a student might have at university.

  • Purpose

  • This project could be used as a tool for prospective or lower year university students to gain insight on how certain types of decision making behaviours could influence aspects of their university experience.
